AI Fitness Testing and Training Assistant in Your Pocket

Core Features

AI Automatic Real-Time Jump Testing

Use millisecond-level AI recognition through your phone camera to automatically measure jump height and explosive power.

AI Automatic Real-Time Barbell Performance Monitoring

Automatically track barbell path with your phone camera, monitor bar speed in real time, and calculate 1RM accurately with the force-velocity method.

AI Smart Posture Assessment

Take a photo to receive a detailed quantitative posture report that accurately identifies posture abnormalities.

100m Sprint Split Timing

Automatically record start, drive, and sprint phases, clearly breaking down speed changes and room for improvement in each segment.

AI-Generated Personalized Training Plans

Combine test data, training goals, and recovery status to intelligently generate phased training plans that fit each athlete's rhythm.

Sport-Specific Fitness Testing and Assessment

Digitally track general and sport-specific fitness from every angle, making each hard-earned improvement visible.

Strength Deficit Analysis

Calculate eccentric utilization ratio (EUR) and dynamic strength index (DSI) to quantify strength gaps and guide targeted training.

Sports Fatigue Monitoring

Identify nervous system and skeletal muscle fatigue through multidimensional data, helping adjust training load and prevent injuries.

Trend Analysis

Track performance trends across dimensions and use periodic curves to identify breakthroughs and plateaus clearly.

Professional Coach Mode

Support multi-athlete management, monitor team status in real time, and bring scientific coaching into a digital workflow.

One-Tap Report Generation

Quickly export test records and professional performance reports, shareable as PDFs to improve communication efficiency.

Hardcore Technology for Smarter Training

Athlimate combines computer vision with sports biomechanics algorithms. With only a phone, it automatically recognizes the body and equipment, completes fitness tests quickly, and generates personalized training plans, making once-expensive lab-level testing available anytime, anywhere.
